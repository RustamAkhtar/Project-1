The Sonic Prime Tv Show

The Sonic Prime TV show is an exciting tv show about Sonic and his friends. In this Sonic and his friends fight the evil Dr.Eggman.The TV show is about how Sonic doesn't listen to his friends and smashes the paradox prism to pieces. Because of this alternate worlds are made where his friends don't know who he is. They have different personalities and he has to assemble the paradox prism to get his friends and thier world back. In the end Sonic succeeds along with the help of his half-enemy shadow who is also a super fast hedgehog.

Sonic

Sonic is a blue hedgehog who can run at the speed of light. He fight against Dr.Eggman for the safety of his beuatiful world. Sonic has an amazing gang of friends who help him in fights against Dr.Eggman and his robot army. When Sonic isn't fight Dr.Eggman he's hanging out at the garage with his friends or arguing with Shadow.

Shadow

Shadow is a black hedgehog who is able to run at lightning speed like Sonic. H e spends a lot of his time running around trying to stop Sonic. When Sonic smashes the paradox prism he remains the same and helps Sonic get thier world back.

Dr.Eggman

Dr.Eggman is cunning evil genius who spends all of his time hatching evil plans and creating robots to destroy Sonic and his friends. When Sonic smashes the paradox prism that he is trying to take it divides Dr.Eggman too. Dr.Eggman always loses.
